Who We Are

We are a community-driven platform designed to connect aspiring students with experienced mentors. Our mission is to make interview preparation simpler, smarter, and more effective by combining real-world experiences with personalized guidance.

At GikSol, we believe that every student deserves access to quality mentorship and guidance. Whether you're preparing for your first job interview or looking to advance your career, we're here to support your journey.
